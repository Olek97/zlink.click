Past halving event dates

  • The first halving event occurred on the 18th of November, 2020 (UTC) at block height 1,046,400 and the reward per block went from 6.25 ZEC to 3.125 ZEC per block.
  • The next Zcash halving is expected to occour in November 2024 at block height 2,092,800 and the block reward will fall from 3.125 ZEC to 1.5625 ZEC per block.
  • The 3rd halving is expected to occour in November 2028 at block height 3,139,200 and the block reward will fall from 1.5625 ZEC to 0.78125 ZEC per block.
  • The 4th halving is expected to occour in November 2032 at block height 4,185,600 and the block reward will fall from 0.78125 to 0.390625 ZEC per block.
